# PRD Operational Addendum

- RAGTruth_Xtended remains the primary tracing substrate for candidate selection, validation, and tracing execution.
- RAGTruth reproduced examples are permitted only as an auxiliary lead source.
- `outputs/examples_registry.csv` is the canonical registry for accepted examples only.
- Auxiliary leads do not enter the registry automatically.
- Pre-registry checks are mandatory before any lead can be admitted.
- If a lead is confidently matched and useful, auxiliary provenance such as temperature may be copied into registry provenance fields.
- RAGTruth generation metadata is provenance and triage support only. It does not replace validation gates and does not guarantee exact reproduction.
- The scientific objective, validation gate, and tracing gate remain unchanged.
